Progression protocol
Double progression rule: Hit top rep in all sets for 2 consecutive sessions → add 2.5 kg next session.

Example: Bench 4×10 completed twice → bump to 4×8 at +2.5 kg. Ride that weight until 4×10 again.

Deload week every 4th week: Cut volume 40%, keep same weights. Non-negotiable — this is where gains are locked in.

Vascularity tip: On arm/shoulder days keep rest under 60s and finish with 2 sets of 20-rep pump work on arms.
